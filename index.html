Botworld Adventure

Botworld Adventure is an open world game. In there you can build your own bot team and create powerful combos.

Each bot is unique, they have their own abbilities and ultimate. You can build the bots by finding the botframe first. Then you can upgrade them with materials which you can find on several maps by opening chest, scavenging for scraps, beating wild bots abd bandits, and so on.

There are many modes that you can play, including offline Adventure, online Arena and Guild Events, etc.
